Benefits of Sustainable Gardening

Benefit Description
Reduces Waste Composting and recycling garden waste can reduce landfill use.
Conserves Water Using drought-resistant plants can conserve water resources.
Improves Air Quality Plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, purifying the air.
Enhances Biodiversity Encourages a variety of species to flourish in your garden.
